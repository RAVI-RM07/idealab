About The Lab

Home / About Lab

Overview

The AICTE IDEA (Idea Development, Evaluation & Application) Lab is established to encourage students for application of science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) fundamentals towards enhanced hands-on experience and learning by doing. The lab acts as a central facility in the campus to make the students more imaginative and creative.

It provides all necessary resources for converting an idea into a prototype. The lab is open 24x7 for students and faculty to work on their projects and products.

Why This Lab?

Encouraging Innovation

To promote a culture of innovation and creative problem solving among the student community.

Skill Enhancement

Training students in 21st-century skills like critical thinking, design thinking, and collaboration.

Industry Ready

Making engineering graduates more employable and industry-ready through practical exposure.
