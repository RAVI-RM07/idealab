Vision & Mission

Home / Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in innovation, design, and prototyping by transforming students into competent professionals with leadership skills and ethical values through experiential learning, research, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

  • To provide state-of-the-art resources and infrastructure for ideation, design, and prototyping.
  • To create a learner-centric environment that promotes hands-on learning and continuous skill development.
  • To strengthen collaboration with industry, research institutions, and innovation ecosystems.
  • To enhance employability and entrepreneurial skills through innovation, startups, and intellectual property creation.
  • To develop sustainable and socially relevant technological solutions by adapting to emerging trends.
